About Us

LIMMA is a platform available to support both upcoming and old farmers in making decisions of particular crops to plant in particular seasons by freely providing the necessary details they require.


The details include the pests, diseases, signs and cures of the specific crops that will help the farmer prevent and cure particular crop pests and diseases to maximise their profits & make informed decisions of which crops to plant in a specific season.


It is all free of charge. Feel free to give us feedback and how we can serve you better.
